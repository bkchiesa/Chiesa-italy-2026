Good to Know
🏛️
Near Your Rome Airbnb
You're right next to the Pantheon! Santa Maria Sopra Minerva is steps away -- free entry, stunning interior, and Michelangelo's Christ Carrying the Cross.
🚗
ZTL Zones
Rome and Florence historic centers have restricted traffic zones. Never drive in -- fines come in the mail months later. Use taxis or walk.
⛪
Campo de' Fiori
Walk through on the way to Trastevere. Site of public executions -- the statue is of Giordano Bruno, burned alive as a heretic. Morning market there too.
💧
Free Water -- Rome
Nasoni fountains throughout Rome give free clean drinking water. Refill your bottles -- it's cold and delicious. Better than anything bottled.
🏰
Valmozzola
The ancestral Chiesa village! Visit Giuseppe Chiesa's home (Strada San Siro 10) and Maria Ferretti's (San Martino 6). A truly special day for your family.
🛒
Fidenza Village
On the drive from Parma to Lake Garda -- designer outlet shopping. Good stop if the group wants it, especially after all that cheese money spent.
🎟️
Buy Tickets Online
Largo Argentina cat sanctuary, Vicus Caprarius, and Colosseum are much cheaper online. Walk-up prices can be double in June.
🍝
Meal Times
Italians eat dinner 8–10pm. Restaurants before 7pm are mostly for tourists. Embrace the rhythm -- afternoon gelato tides you over.
